About Us

Company History & Achievements

In the midst of the great momentum and the multiple products gathered under one roof in the Yiwu International Market – the largest wholesale market in the world with millions of different items – just a few hundred meters from that international market, Al-Ruqi Trading Co., Ltd. was founded in Yiwu in early 2006. Since then, it has contributed to strengthening cross‑border trade links to many countries around the world with stability, credibility, and diligent work in selecting goods that suit the desires of consumers, wholesalers, and international markets. It started with various Middle Eastern markets and gradually expanded to cover areas and countries around the world from different continents. Our company has successfully surpassed twenty years thanks to our excellence in honesty, integrity, and commitment to providing high‑quality, transparent, and fair services. This has kept our clients with us for up to 18 consecutive years, receiving excellent services either from the Yiwu International Market or from specialized factories throughout China to select quality, price, and sufficient guarantees to meet our clients' desires and enhance their competitiveness in their local markets.

Company branches were registered in Hong Kong in 2013 and in England in 2021 under the unified name (Al-Taqaddum Trading Co., Ltd.). As part of the company's expansion and global openness, in 2024 it opened its first branch in Kano, Nigeria, under the name (Al-Taqaddum Trading Co., Ltd. – Kano), the same name as the Hong Kong and England branches. The Kano market is one of the most important and largest emerging markets in Southwest Africa and is under construction to become the largest commercial and economic center in Africa. Our company thus becomes a new trade hub serving many countries in the region with goods that meet the needs of local and foreign markets in Southwest and Central Africa, bearing the trademark registered as KPT. Our product quickly gained the trust of consumers in various African markets.

In addition to our large volume of business in North and Northwest African markets (Tunisia, Algeria, Morocco) with which we have maintained excellent trade relations for more than eighteen years.
